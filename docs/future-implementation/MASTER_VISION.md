## ðŸŽ¨ Product Principles

### 1. Social First
Education is inherently social. We put community and collaboration at the center of the learning experience.

### 2. Mobile First
Design for mobile devices first, then scale up to desktop. Most students access learning on phones.

### 3. Engagement Driven
Use game mechanics, social features, and interactive content to keep students engaged and motivated.

### 4. Data Informed
Make decisions based on data. Track everything, analyze patterns, optimize continuously.

### 5. Accessible to All
Ensure the platform is usable by everyone, regardless of ability, device, or internet speed.

### 6. Privacy Centric
Protect user data rigorously. Build trust through transparency and security.

### 7. Globally Local
Think globally but act locally. Support local languages, cultures, and education systems.
